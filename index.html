Interactive Lab

Change parameters. Watch the behavior change.

These browser-side demos are lightweight conceptual models inspired by stochastic control, density evolution, orbital uncertainty, and robust correction.

Covariance Steering Playground

Particles are guided from a dispersed initial cloud toward a desired terminal covariance. Increase control strength to contract uncertainty faster; increase diffusion to perturb the flow.

Fokker–Planck Density Flow

A particle density moves through a nonlinear vector field. The potential strength bends the flow; diffusion controls the stochastic spreading.

Orbital Uncertainty Propagation

A cloud of nearby orbital states propagates around a nominal orbit. Perturbation strength stretches the cloud, showing how uncertainty grows along the trajectory.

Robust Midcourse Correction

Compare an uncertain trajectory against a corrected one. Higher correction gain pulls the trajectory into the terminal gate, but saturation limits the achievable response.
