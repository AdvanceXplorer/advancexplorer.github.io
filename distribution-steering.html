Research topic

Distribution Steering

Control is not only about moving trajectories. In many aerospace and autonomy problems, the object being controlled is an entire probability law.

Keywords: covariance steering, Fokker–Planck equations, optimal transport, free-final-time steering, measure-valued dynamics.

Problem

Design controls that transform an initial uncertainty distribution into a desired terminal distribution under stochastic dynamics.

Methods

Covariance steering, nonlinear optimal transport, Fokker–Planck transformations, stochastic maximum principle, and numerical optimal control.

Applications

Spacecraft guidance, robust trajectory design, robot autonomy, uncertainty-aware planning, and safety-critical decision making.

Current directions

  • Continuous-time covariance steering with common free-final time.
  • Nonlinear distribution steering through Fokker–Planck and optimal transport formulations.
  • Free-final-time transversality conditions for distribution-level control.
  • Numerical methods for steering non-Gaussian distributions under nonlinear dynamics.
