Distribution steering

Distribution steering asks how to design controls that move an entire probability distribution from an initial law to a desired terminal law. This is the natural language for autonomy when uncertainty, dispersion, and stochasticity are central rather than secondary.

Problems I care about

  • Continuous-time covariance steering with common or free terminal time.
  • Nonlinear distribution steering through Fokker-Planck or Perron-Frobenius representations.
  • Non-Gaussian steering with Gaussian mixtures, particles, score functions, or feature-based approximations.
  • Turnpike and asymptotic structure in small- and large-time horizons.
