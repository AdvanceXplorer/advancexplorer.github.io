Research topic

Stochastic Optimal Control

My work in stochastic control focuses on stochastic optimal control, stochastic optimal stopping, robust/adaptive policies, MPPI/path-integral control, and sparse optimization methods for autonomy under uncertainty.

Technical direction

Control laws that remain meaningful when the model is uncertain.

The goal is to combine theoretical optimality structure with computational algorithms that can be validated under dispersions, rollout failures, and model mismatch.

Path-integral and MPPI methods

Costate-informed rollouts, sparse kernel features, optimal-transport MPPI, and distributional constraints.

Optimal stopping

Free-final-time and stopping-boundary problems for stochastic systems and distributional objectives.

Robust control

Contraction metrics, structured robust control, adaptive updates, and validation through Monte Carlo and replay analysis.
