Research topic

Stochastic Optimal Control

I study stochastic control problems where optimality, uncertainty, stopping, and computational structure all interact.

Keywords: stochastic calculus, optimal stopping, path-integral control, MPPI, costates, sparse nonlinear programming.

Optimality structure

Stochastic maximum principle, Hamiltonian viewpoints, costate dynamics, transversality, and free-final-time conditions.

Sampling control

MPPI and path-integral methods with costate-informed rollouts, sparse features, and optimal-transport-inspired corrections.

Stopping and recovery

Optimal stopping, stochastic recovery policies, diffusion/score viewpoints, and robustness under non-Gaussian uncertainties.

Questions I care about

  • How can stochastic optimal control be made computationally useful for nonlinear, high-dimensional systems?
  • How can costate information improve sampling-based control?
  • How should free terminal time be treated for distribution-level stochastic control?
  • How can sparse structure be exploited without losing uncertainty information?
