Research topic

Astrodynamics and Aerospace Autonomy

My aerospace work connects launch-vehicle GN&C, re-entry trajectory optimization, orbital rendezvous, spacecraft autonomy, and uncertainty-aware mission design.

Application domain

Safety-critical motion under uncertainty.

Aerospace systems are an ideal testbed for stochastic control because uncertainty, constraints, and verification requirements are unavoidable.

Trajectory optimization

Pseudospectral transcription, sparse nonlinear programming, endpoint regions, and robust optimization under dispersions.

Launch and re-entry

Launch-vehicle guidance, robust re-entry optimization, aerodynamic uncertainty, and Monte Carlo validation.

Spacecraft autonomy

Rendezvous, docking, formation reconfiguration, orbital insertion, Lambert solvers, and onboard correction logic.
