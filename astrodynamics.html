Research topic

Astrodynamics and GN&C

My applied background is in launch vehicles, re-entry, rendezvous, spacecraft mission design, and robust guidance/control.

Keywords: trajectory optimization, orbital insertion, rendezvous, re-entry, ensemble pseudospectral control, Monte Carlo validation.

Trajectory optimization

Robust and sparse nonlinear programming methods for launch, re-entry, rendezvous, and multi-phase trajectory design.

Guidance and control

Real-time guidance, robust autopilots, control allocation, structured H∞ ideas, contraction metrics, and recursive replanning.

Uncertainty

Monte Carlo validation, polynomial chaos, unscented methods, Gaussian mixtures, and uncertainty propagation for aerospace systems.

Applied systems

  • Launch vehicle configuration, mission, and trajectory design.
  • Re-entry trajectory optimization with endpoint-region constraints.
  • Close-proximity rendezvous and space servicing vehicle guidance.
  • Formation reconfiguration and uncertainty-aware orbital control.
