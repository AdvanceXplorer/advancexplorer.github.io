Astrodynamics and guidance

My applied background is in launch-vehicle and spacecraft GN&C, trajectory optimization, rendezvous/docking, re-entry, robust guidance, and mission/system design.

Applied interests

  • Trajectory and configuration design for launch vehicles and spacecraft missions.
  • Uncertainty-aware re-entry trajectory optimization and endpoint-region constraints.
  • Close-proximity rendezvous, formation reconfiguration, and robust recursive replanning.
  • Monte Carlo validation, PCE/unscented methods, and log/replay analysis for verification.

Research connection

Astrodynamics provides a natural testbed for distribution steering: uncertainty evolves geometrically, dynamics are structured, and safety margins are not optional. This makes it ideal for sparse, stochastic, and robust optimization methods.
