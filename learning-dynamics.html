Research topic

Learning Dynamics and Validation

This direction studies data-driven dynamics, Koopman/Perron–Frobenius models, rollout analysis, VLA/LBM evaluation, sparse interpretability, and validation/verification for autonomous systems.

Goal

Understand what learned policies actually do.

The focus is not only learning policies, but also evaluating their failure modes, robustness, dynamics, and sparse structure.

Data-driven dynamics

Koopman/Perron–Frobenius approximations, model-learning pipelines, uncertainty propagation, and control-oriented validation.

Large behavior models

Vision-language-action models, large behavior models, diffusion-style policies, capability evaluation, and failure-mode analysis.

Sparse interpretability

Optimization-based tools for finding influential features, action chunks, rollout patterns, and policy failure mechanisms.
