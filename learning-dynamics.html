Research topic

Learning Dynamics and Validation

I am interested in learning dynamics from data while preserving the control, robustness, and verification questions that matter in autonomy.

Keywords: Koopman, Perron–Frobenius, VLA/LBM evaluation, data-driven dynamics, policy failure modes, sim-to-real validation.

Operators

Koopman and Perron–Frobenius viewpoints for learning and propagating dynamics, distributions, and features.

Behavior models

Evaluation of large behavior models and VLA policies through rollout analysis, capability diagnostics, and failure-mode identification.

Verification

Robust validation, log replay, test infrastructure, and uncertainty-aware stress testing for learned autonomy.

Research angle

The goal is not only to learn a predictive model, but to understand which learned representations are useful for control, robustness, and safety.
